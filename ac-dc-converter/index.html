1. Forward converter

1.1. One-switch sigle-ended forward converter (isolated buck converter)

This is essentially a buck converter with transformer inserted.

We want to operate in Continuous Conduction Mode. $I_{Lout}$ never reach zero.

In DCM we may have peak detect phenomina, we $V_{out}^{'} = (3-4) \times V_{out}$

ESR is from $C_{OUT}$, and $\Delta I$ is effected by the design of $L_{out}$. Rule of thumb is $\Delta I$ = 10% to 40% of full load.
